Technical Implementation & Reproducibility
Technology Stack: odc-stac Python module + STAC endpoint + xarray.Datasets

Data Access: Direct access to Digital Earth Africa's earth observation data via STAC endpoint, enabling loading of water extent, Sentinel-1, and Sentinel-2 data as xarray.Datasets.

Deployment Flexibility: Works locally or on cloud platforms (AWS) using open standards.

Benefits: Streamlined workflow, Full portability, Reproducible methods, Adoptable by other researchers/agencies across Africa
